Tourist Safety Guidelines

Follow these safety tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable trip. Stay alert and prepared at all times.

General Safety Tips

  • ✔ Always carry a copy of your ID and emergency contacts.
  • ✔ Avoid traveling alone at night in unfamiliar areas.
  • ✔ Keep your valuables in a secure place.
  • ✔ Respect local customs and rules.
  • ✔ Stay hydrated and follow basic health precautions.

Local Guidelines

  • 📌 Follow all traffic rules and pedestrian signals.
  • 📌 Avoid restricted areas and private properties.
  • 📌 Report any suspicious activity to authorities.
